## Key Insights & Recommendations

- Most employees show **neutral or positive sentiment**, indicating general engagement.  
- Certain employees are flagged as **flight risks**; management may need to address their concerns.  
- **Top positive employees** demonstrate high engagement and could be recognized for contributions.  
- **Predictive modeling** shows sentiment is strongly influenced by message counts and content.  
- Recommendation: Monitor employees with repeated negative sentiment patterns and implement **engagement programs** for at-risk employees.
